Sath Villa Ayurvedic Resort Web App

Sath Villa is a web-based platform that aims to digitize and simplify the process of booking Ayurvedic wellness treatments and buying herbal products in Sri Lanka. It tackles the problems of traditional manual booking systems used by Ayurvedic resorts and wellness centers. Sath Villa offers a centralized and easy-to-use digital solution that links local service providers with tourists, both from Sri Lanka and abroad.

The system allows users to easily browse wellness packages, book treatments online, make safe payments through the Stripe payment gateway, and get instant confirmations and digital receipts. Besides treatment bookings, the platform features an online marketplace where users can find and buy genuine Ayurvedic wellness products directly from trusted sources.

Created as a full-stack MERN application (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), Sath Villa includes strong authentication, role-based access control, and a user-friendly admin dashboard for managing bookings, payments, and reviews. The project followed agile software development principles, which helped in making ongoing improvements, assigning tasks efficiently, and gathering continuous feedback from users.

The platform went through organized steps of requirement analysis, system design, database modeling, and multi-level testing. It was assessed for usability, performance, and reliability. The results show a high potential for improving accessibility, transparency, and efficiency in Sri Lanka's Ayurveda and wellness tourism sector. Future plans involve adding AI-driven chat support, better analytics dashboards, and mobile optimization to boost user engagement and extend service reach.
